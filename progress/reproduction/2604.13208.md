## 3. Hydrodynamic frames — Table tab:frames

Viscous parametrizations (Eq. eq:viscous_parametrizations), with lengthscale L_0 and dimensionless η̂, τ̂; ζ=0 throughout (bulk viscosity does not affect axial sector):

- **Parametrization A** (Eq. par_a):  η = η̂ (ρ+p) L_0 c_s²,  θ = L_0 η̂,  τ = τ̂ L_0 η̂
- **Parametrization B** (Eq. par_b):  η = η̂ p L_0,  θ = L_0 p/ρ,  τ = τ̂ L_0 p/ρ

Causality conditions:
- A: η̂≥0, τ̂>0, 0 ≤ c_s² ≤ τ̂/(2+τ̂)
- B: 0≤η̂≤3/4, τ̂ > max(η̂, 2/(1-c_s²))

Second-sound speed: c_η² = η/[τ(ρ+p)]. For parametrization A: c_η² = c_s²/τ̂.

**Table tab:frames (verbatim):**

| Frame | Parametrization | τ̂ | max c_s² |
|-------|-----------------|-----|----------|
| A1 | A (Eq. par_a) | 10 | 0.83 |
| A2 | A (Eq. par_a) | 20 | 0.91 |
| B1 | B (Eq. par_b) | 10 | 0.8 |
| B2 | B (Eq. par_b) | 20 | 0.9 |

max c_s² = maximum sound speed for which causality is guaranteed.

NOTE on η̂ vs η_c: the tables are indexed by the *central* shear viscosity η_c = η(r=0) in cgs (g cm⁻¹ s⁻¹). Text states η_c ~ 10^{29-32} cgs corresponds approximately to η̂ ~ 10^{-3}–1. The exact map from η_c to η̂ depends on L_0 and the parametrization; L_0 numeric value is NOT given explicitly in the source (introduced symbolically). To reproduce the table, one fixes η_c at r=0 (which fixes η̂·L_0 via the parametrization given ρ_c) rather than η̂ directly.

KSS bound estimate for ultracompact objects: η ≳ 5.4×10^31 cgs (M_⊙/M).

## 6. η-mode family — Figure fig:eta_modes (complex_plane_2.pdf)

**FIGURE ONLY — no numeric table in source.**

- Complex plane: horizontal axis = frequency (real part / f), vertical axis = damping time. Color scale = central viscosity η_c (lighter = less viscous). Two hydrodynamic frames (blue, red).
- w-modes = circles; perfect-fluid limit = star marker. η-modes = triangles.
- η-modes: new family, restoring force from shear viscosity + regulator τ̂; no perfect-fluid counterpart.
- **Onset/limit behavior:** generically Im ω → 0 as η_c → 0 (η-modes become undamped in perfect-fluid limit). They were NOT tracked down to very small viscosity.
- η-modes oscillate at kHz frequencies, damping times of order ms (comparable to w-modes).
- **Mode avoidance:** w- and η-mode families approach as shear viscosity increases but never cross (repel). This drives the B1 discrepancy in Table tab:modes_frames at large η_c.
- NOTE: no explicit (f, τ) numbers for η-modes are given anywhere in the text/tables — all η-mode info is qualitative or figure-only.

## 7. Ultracompact constant-density stars — Figure fig:ucos (plot_ultracompact.pdf)

**FIGURE ONLY — no numeric table in source.**

- Constant-density (ρ=const) stars, increasing compactness M/R (up to Buchdahl limit M/R ≤ 4/9 ≈ 0.444). All compact enough to have a stable lightring.
- Frame **B** parametrization only.
- Fundamental w-mode real & imaginary parts vs harmonic number ℓ, for different compactness.
- Solid lines = ideal (perfect fluid); dotted = viscous with η_c = 10^31 g cm⁻¹ s⁻¹.

## 8. Governing equations (for the reproduction code)

Coupled ODE system for (ψ, Z), ψ = e^{(ν-λ)/2} h_r / r, δu^A ∝ Z(r)/r² (Eqs. eq:gw_eq, eq:fluid_eq):

- f[d(fψ')]' + (ω²−V)ψ = −16π e^{ν/2} iω η ψ + C_1 Z   (gw_eq)
- f[d(fZ')]' + (c_η^{-2} ω²−U)Z = C_2 Z' + C_3 Z + C_4 ψ' + C_5 ψ   (fluid_eq)

with f² = e^{ν-λ}, RW potential V = e^ν[ℓ(ℓ+1)/r² − 6m/r³ + 4π(ρ−p)] (Eq. RW_potential_star), c_η² = η/[τ(p+ρ)].
U, C_1..C_5 given in widetext block (lines 299-308). Surface regularity condition Eq. eq:surface with frame-dependent coefficients B_i^{(A)}, B_i^{(B)} (lines 335-353), C = M/R.

Exterior: vacuum RW equation, V = f[ℓ(ℓ+1)/r² − 6M/r³], f = 1−2M/r. Outgoing BC ψ ~ e^{iωr*}, r* = r + 2M log(r/2M − 1).

Method: in-solution integrated from r_min~0 with two regular seeds (ψ~r^{ℓ+1}); up-solution via Benhar continued-fraction / Leaver 4-term→3-term recurrence (matching radius a, 4M < a < 2R < 2a); QNM = roots of Wronskian Δ(ω)=ψ_in ψ_up' − ψ_up ψ_in' = 0. Implemented in Mathematica.
